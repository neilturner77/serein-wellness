Common Questions

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

Yes. A medical consultation is required before any treatment — especially those involving prescription products. This ensures your safety, suitability, and an outcome that feels natural and balanced.

Who performs the treatments?

All treatments are carried out by a qualified and insured medical professional with training in aesthetics.

Will my results look natural?

Yes. The approach at Serein Wellness is subtle and refined — designed to refresh, not change, your appearance.

Is there any downtime?

Most treatments involve little to no downtime. You may experience mild redness or swelling, which usually settles within a few hours or days depending on the procedure.

How long do results last?

Results vary depending on the treatment and individual response. Your practitioner will discuss this during your consultation and create a personalised plan for maintenance.

Is my information kept confidential?

Always. All consultations and records are handled securely and in accordance with medical confidentiality standards.

What if I'm unsure which treatment is right for me?

That's what the consultation is for. You'll receive clear, honest advice with no obligation to proceed — the goal is to find what's genuinely best for you.

Do you offer a prescribing service?

Yes, we offer a prescribing service to both medical and non-medical aesthetic practitioners.
